On-Page SEO (20)

Content Quality (20)

- Is there depth or just marketing fluff?

Authority Signals (20)
ONLY count if explicitly visible:

- Case studies
- Press mentions
If none visible → low score

UX & Conversion (20)
Check:
- Clear CTA
- Navigation clarity
- Friction (clutter, confusion)
